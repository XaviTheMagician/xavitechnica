Laptop Maintenance

Expert diagnosis and repair for all your laptop issues.

Understanding Laptop Maintenance

Laptops are essential tools for work, study, and entertainment. Regular maintenance and timely repair of common issues can significantly extend their lifespan and ensure optimal performance. At IT Technica, we provide comprehensive laptop maintenance and repair services to keep your device running smoothly.

Common Laptop Problems We Fix

No Display

Troubleshooting and fixing issues where your laptop screen remains black or shows no output.

Hanging & Slow Performance

Optimizing system speed, resolving freezing issues, and upgrading components for better performance.

Application Problems

Diagnosing and fixing software crashes, incompatibility, and other application-related errors.

Dim Display

Repairing backlight issues, screen flickering, or overall dimness of your laptop screen.

Frequent Restarting

Addressing issues causing unexpected shutdowns or continuous restarts of your laptop.

Charging Problems & No Backup

Fixing battery charging issues and problems with battery life and backup capacity.

Charger Problems

Repair or replacement of faulty laptop chargers and power adapters.

Windows Activation

Assistance with Windows operating system activation issues and licensing.

Wi-Fi Problems

Diagnosing and resolving wireless connectivity issues, network adapter problems.

Feeding/Scrolling Screen

Addressing issues with screen tearing, erratic scrolling, or display distortion.

Touchpad Problems

Fixing unresponsive, erratic, or non-functional laptop touchpads.

Camera Problems

Resolving issues with integrated webcams not working or showing poor quality.

Keyboard Problems

Repairing sticky keys, unresponsive keys, or complete keyboard malfunctions.

Heating Problems

Addressing overheating issues due to dust, poor ventilation, or faulty cooling systems.

Touch Screen Problems

Repairing unresponsive or inaccurate touch screen functionality on compatible laptops.

Booting Problems

Diagnosing and resolving issues preventing your laptop from starting up correctly.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How often should I get my laptop serviced?

A: We recommend a professional service at least once a year to keep your laptop in optimal condition and prevent major issues.

Q: How long does a typical laptop repair take?

A: Repair times vary depending on the complexity of the issue. Simple repairs might take a few hours, while more complex ones could take a few days. We'll provide an estimate after diagnosis.

Q: Do you offer warranty on repairs?

A: Yes, we offer a warranty on our repair services and replaced parts. Please inquire for specific terms and conditions.

Q: Can you recover data from a damaged laptop?

A: In many cases, yes. We offer data recovery services for damaged hard drives or non-booting systems. Contact us for an assessment.
